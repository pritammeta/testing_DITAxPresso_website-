FAQs

You can visit our FAQ section that may have a few answers to the questions you are looking for.

1. Can we customize DITA maps? How can we work with DITA Maps ?
Yes, you can customize the DITA maps in DITAxPresso. DITAxPresso will create a copy of your imported DITA maps and allow you to restructure the map copy.
2. We have DITA maps prepared in other tools. Can we import them to DITAxPresso ?
Yes, DITAxPresso lets you work easily with imported DITA Maps. As long as they have a .ditamap filetype, they can be used in DITAxPresso.
3. Can I create DITA topics from scratch ?
The current version does not support the creation of new DITA topics. Although we are introducing this feature in DITAxPresso version 3.0 soon.
